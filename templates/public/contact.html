About Our Process

Barnacle Ballast Inc. is committed to transparent, collaborative filmmaking. We welcome inquiries from press, potential collaborators, and anyone interested in learning more about independent film production.

Our current focus is on "Creatures in the Tall Grass," an intimate character study that explores family dynamics in a rural setting. We're always interested in hearing from talented individuals who share our passion for authentic storytelling.

  • Creative collaborators
  • Unique filming locations
  • Technical expertise
  • Distribution opportunities
